About Us

Friday, 25th July, 1986 saw the birth of The International Women's Association now officially known as IWA. Founded by Nirmal Seshadri and Nancy Stempel (Wife of the American Consul General), IWA has grown from 7 founding members to a vibrant organization of almost 300 members from all over the world.

IWA is a multi-cultural organization whose objectives are to promote friendship and a greater understanding among women of different, faiths, cultures and countries. IWA provides a platform for the exchange of ideas and the celebration of diversity. IWA is a non-profit organization not actively engaged in fund raising.

The structure of IWA with a ratio of 3 Indians to 1 non-Indian gives an international flavour,a lively melting point!
