About Us

Friday, 25th July, 1986 saw the birth of The International Women's Association now officially known as IWC. Founded by Nirmal Seshadri and Nancy Stempel (Wife of the American Consul General), IWC has grown from 7 founding members to a vibrant organization of almost 300 members from all over the world.

IWC is a multi-cultural organization whose objectives are to promote friendship and a greater understanding among women of different, faiths, cultures and countries. IWC provides a platform for the exchange of ideas and the celebration of diversity. IWC is a non-profit organization not actively engaged in fund raising.

The structure of IWC with a ratio of 3 Indians to 1 non-Indian gives an international flavour,a lively melting point!
